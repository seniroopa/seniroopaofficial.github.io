about seniroopa

seniroopa is a company that aims to promote the art creations from various artists around Indonesia and to help them sell their creations to buyers who are interested. Since the pandemic started many art creators has difficulty in selling their creations and they will find it difficult to promote their creation anywhere because the lack of audience and our business aims to help them sell it by promoting their creations constantly and if the creation is sold we will receive 5% from the profit of the artist that was received from selling their creation, we also sell the material that is usually needed to start creations such as brushes, acrylic paint, oil paint, modeling paste and many more . We also offer webinars that can help you improve your skills in art creation and hopefully boost the artists creativity and skills that can be learned    .
